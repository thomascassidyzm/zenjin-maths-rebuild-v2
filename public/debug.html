Debug Controls

This page loads the minimal player with injected debug controls. Look for the debug panel in the bottom right:

  • Complete 20/20: Complete current stitch with perfect score
  • Complete 10/20: Complete with partial score (40 total points)
  • Cycle to Next Tube: Move to next tube (1→2→3→1)

The page will reload after each action to show the changes.

Alt+R to reload with a fresh state
